The P.P. Dice

Rules of P.Pig.Dice:
  1. Write your names on the input form then touch start button
  2. Both participants can roll the dice 6times
  3. When you roll the dice and it falls on one then you lose and no points will be counted
  4. When you roll the dice the points will count on the count box when you press End button to switch to your partner
